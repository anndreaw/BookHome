Quality books at cheap prices!

Brand new books are often costly to many book lovers with limited resources. Thus, our aim is to help provide quality books at an affordable price. Our company ensures that the condition of books are carefully examined and graded accordingly before sold. The books here are all donated or sold from bookstores or even our customers! If you wish to donate or sell any books contact us below!

Graded carefully

  • Like New
    Books that are either not opened before or are in mint condition. No markings or stains in the pages.
  • Good Condition
    Books that shows small signs of wear. Spine might be slightly creased with minimal stains. Pages are all intact. May show light tanning or foxing on the pages.
  • Acceptable Condition
    Books that have been well read. All pages are intact with a creased spine. May show medium to heavy tanning or foxing of pages but does not interfere with readability.
